Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Italian immigrant Giovanni Rossi, Tobch Heaven began as a small family-owned pizzeria in the heart of the city. Giovanni brought with him generations-old recipes from Naples, the birthplace of pizza.

Today, we still use the same traditional methods - hand-stretched dough, slow-fermented for 48 hours, San Marzano tomatoes, and fresh mozzarella di bufala imported weekly from Italy.

What sets us apart is our wood-fired oven that reaches 900°F, cooking our pizzas in just 90 seconds for that perfect char and crispiness that pizza lovers crave.
